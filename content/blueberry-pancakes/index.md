American style pancakes, best served with blueberries or another fruit.

## Ingredients

- 30g of unsalted butter
- 2 Large eggs
- 200g plain flour
- 2 1/2 tsp of baking powder
- 20g of caster sugar
- 250ml of buttermilk
- 1 tsp of vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Melt butter
1. Lightly Beat eggs together
1. Sift flour and baking powder together
1. Stir in sugar with flour till evenly mixed
1. Add milk and vanilla to the eggs and lightly beat together till well blended
1. Form a well in the center of the dry ingredients
1. Pour the mix in to the well slowly in small additions and mix well after each addition
1. Then Whisk in the butter till smooth
1. Melt butter in a non stick pan over medium heat
1. Put a serving in the pan
1. Before flipping add any fruit, blueberry, strawberry, raspberry or Banana to each pancake then flip.
1. Cook evenly on both sides
1. Cook for 1-2 min till evenly brown
