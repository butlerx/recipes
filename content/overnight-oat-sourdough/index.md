Easy to make and super soft and tasty sourdough loaf

**Note:** Prepare the night before you plan to Bake

## Ingredients

## Directions

- Soak 100g of porridge oats in 180g of water, leave in the fridge
- Make an autolyse from 400g bread flour and 275g of water.
- Leave both for 12 hours
- Mix 10g of salt in to the autolyse, then add oats and 150 g of sourdough
  starter.
- kneed it all together to make sure oats distributed well and dough is supple.
  Leave for 4 hours to rise.
- Pre shape the dough in to 2 loafs and rest for another 20-30 min
- then on a floured work surface shape in to a batard and place in to a bake tin
  to rise for another 2 hours.
- cover dough in porridge oats.
- Pre heat oven to 250c
- Once hot enough put bread in for 20-30 minutes. Put a oven safe pan of water
  in the base of the oven to generate steam.
- Once the loaf is the right colour remove from the tin and place back in the
  oven for a further 10 minute.
- let it cool for 30 minutes on a wire rack before cutting in
