I grew up with a large number of cooking apple trees in the garden, which meant I spent a lot of time eating apple
scones.

## Ingredients

- 250g self-raising flour
- 60g unsalted butter
- 2 tbs of caster sugar
- 4 tbs of milk
- 2 small cooking apples, grated

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 220 Celsius
1. Cut the butter into cubes and keep in the freezer before using.
1. Sieve flour into a bowl
1. Cut-Rub the butter into flour until it's like breadcrumbs.
1. Add the apple and sugar and mix.
1. Add the milk and mix into a ball
1. Roll out the mixture on a floured surface.
1. Cut circles out with a cutter and place them on a greased baking tray.
1. Brush down with milk.
1. Bake for 10 minutes in the oven or until golden
1. Allow to cool on a wire rack
