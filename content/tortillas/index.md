Home made tortillas. Great for tacos or making in to torilla chips.

## Ingredients

- 100g Masa Harina flour
- 150ml of boiling water

## Directions

1. Mix the flour and water in a bowl
1. Continue to add water a little at a time until you get a smooth dough that
   holds its shape - Add more flour if its to sticky
1. Divide the dough in to four small, even balls
1. On a counter top covered in parchment paper, place the ball under another
   piece of parchment and press flat with a frying pan.
1. The tortilla should be 10 - 12 cm and few millimeter tick
1. Heat a dry frying pan over high heat
1. Place the tortilla on the pan and cook for 2 minutes on each side on
1. Allow to cool before serving
