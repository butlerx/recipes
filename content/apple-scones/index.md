I grew up with a large number of cooking apple trees in the garden, which meant I spent a lot of time eating apple
scones.

## Ingredients

- 250g self-raising flour
- 60g unsalted butter
- 2 tbs of caster sugar
- 4 tbs of milk
- 2 small cooking apples, grated

## Directions

1.  Preheat oven to 220 Celsius
2.  Cut the butter into cubes and keep in the freezer before using.
3.  Sieve flour into a bowl
4.  Cut-Rub the butter into flour until it's like breadcrumbs.
5.  Add the apple and sugar and mix.
6.  Add the milk and mix into a ball
7.  Roll out the mixture on a floured surface.
8.  Cut circles out with a cutter and place them on a greased baking tray.
9.  Brush down with milk.
10. Bake for 10 minutes in the oven or until golden
11. Allow to cool on a wire rack
