---
title: Blueberry Pancakes
date: 2019-12-23
category: Breakfast
layout: recipe
tags:
  - Fruit
yield: 12
prepTime: 15
cookTime: 7
cuisine: American
directions:
  - Melt butter
  - Lightly Beat eggs together
  - Sift flour and baking powder together
  - Stir in sugar with flour till evenly mixed
  - Add milk and vanilla to the eggs and lightly beat together till well blended
  - Form a well in the center of the dry ingredients
  - Pour the mix in to the well slowly in small additions and mix well after
    each addition
  - Then Whisk in the butter till smooth
  - Melt butter in a non stick pan over medium heat
  - Put a serving in the pan
  - Before flipping add any fruit, blueberry, strawberry, raspberry or Banana to
    each pancake then flip.
  - Cook evenly on both sides
  - Cook for 1-2 min till evenly brown
ingredients:
  - 30g of unsalted butter
  - 2 Large eggs
  - 200g plain flour
  - 2 1/2 tsp of baking powder
  - 20g of caster sugar
  - 250ml of buttermilk
  - 1 tsp of vanilla extract
---

American style pancakes, best served with blueberries or another fruit.
