---
title: Walnut Bread
date: 2019-12-23
layout: recipe
tags:
  - Bread
  - Vegetarian
cuisine: Irish
yield: 2
prepTime: 15
cookTime: 50
ingredients:
  - 225g Plain Flour
  - 225g Extra Coarse Wholemeal Flour
  - 56g Jumbo Porridge Oats
  - 1 tsp Salt
  - 56g Sugar
  - 3 tsp Baking Powder
  - 1 ½ tsp Bread Soda
  - 1 Whole Egg
  - 600ml Milk
  - 2 Handfuls Walnuts
  - 100ml Black Treacle
directions:
  - pre-heat oven to 160°c
  - Mix all of the dry ingredients together
  - Whisk milk and eggs together
  - Add milk, eggs, walnuts and treacle to dry mix and mix well
  - Grease your tin lightly and line the bottom of the tin with parchment paper
  - Place the mixture into the tin
  - Scatter Porridge Oats over top
  - Bake for approximately 50 minutes
  - Turn out on to a wire rack to cool
