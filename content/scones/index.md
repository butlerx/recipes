Family, scones recipes. Best eaten hot

## Ingredients

- 250g self raisingflour
- 60g unsalted butter
- 2 TBS of caster sugar
- 4 TBS of milk
- 2 small cooking apples, grated

## directions

1. preheat oven to 220 Celcius
1. cut butter in to cubes and keep in freezer before using.
1. Sieve flour in to a bowl
1. rub butter in to flour, until its like breadcrumbs.
1. Add the apple and sugar and mix.
1. Add the milk and mix in to a ball
1. Roll out the mixture on a floured surface.
1. Cut circles out with a cutter and place on greesed baking tray.
1. Brush down with milk.
1. Bake for 10 minutes in the oven, or until golden
1. Allow to cool on a wire rack
