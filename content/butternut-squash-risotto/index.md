https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/butternut-squash-sage-risotto

A few changes from the bbc recipe, remove the parmesan, chop up the squash much
smaller, add some peas near the end and stir in some butter just before serving
as a side dish with roast duck or lamb chops. It\'s lighter and a better side
dish without the cheese
