yield: 6
prepTime: 25
cookTime: 100
ingredients:
  - 45g unsalted butter
  - 28g all-purpose flour
  - Black pepper to taste
  - 475ml milk
  - 128g shredded cheddar cheese
  - 6 medium thinly sliced potatoes
  - 2 Red onion, diced
directions:
  - Preheat oven to 350Â° C
  - In a large saucepan, melt butter over low heat.
  - Stir in flour and pepper until smooth.
  - Gradually add milk.
  - cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
  - Remove from heat and stir in cheese until melted.
  - Add potatoes and onion.
  - Transfer to a baking dish, cover and bake 1 hour.
  - Uncover and make for another bake 30-40 minutes or until the potatoes are
    tender.
