Simple Potato Au Gratin

## Ingredients

- 45g unsalted butter
- 28g all-purpose flour
- Black pepper to taste
- 475ml milk
- 128g shredded cheddar cheese
- 6 medium thinly sliced potatoes
- 2 Red onion, diced

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350Â° C
1. In a large saucepan, melt butter over low heat.
1. Stir in flour and pepper until smooth.
1. Gradually add milk.
1. cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
1. Remove from heat and stir in cheese until melted.
1. Add potatoes and onion.
1. Transfer to a baking dish, cover and bake for 1 hour.
1. Uncover and make for another bake for 30-40 minutes or until the potatoes are tender.
