## Ingredients

- 2 kg Left over Ham from holidays
- 1 bottle Red wine
- 0.5L Cream
- Chili
- Curry powder

## Directions

1. Slice ham into baking dish.
1. Pour wine over so that it almost covers the ham.
1. Soak for half a day or so.
1. Add the spices.
1. Pour over cream so that the ham is covered (you were a bit cheap on the wine, right?).
1. Execute in oven at ~200C for 45-60 mins.

Enjoy with stomped or riced potatoes
