ingredients:
  - 100g Masa Harina flour
  - 150ml of cold water
directions:
  - Mix the flour and water in a bowl
  - Continue to add water a little at a time until you get a smooth dough that
    holds its shape - Add more flour if its to sticky
  - Divide the dough in to four small, even balls
  - On a counter top covered in parchment paper, place the ball under another
    piece of parchment and press flat with a frying pan.
  - The tortilla should be 10 - 12 cm and few millimeter tick
  - Heat a dry frying pan over high heat
  - Place the tortilla on the pan and cook for 2 minutes on each side on
  - Allow to cool before serving
---

Home made tortillas. Great for tacos or making in to torilla chips.
