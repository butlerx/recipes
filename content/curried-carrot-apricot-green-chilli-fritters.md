## Ingredients

- 400g peeled and grated carrot
- ½ tsp salt
- 125g gram flour (chick pea flour)
- ½ tsp baking powder
- 6 dried apricots, finely sliced
- 1 medium green chilli, deseeded and finely diced
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp curry powder
- ½ tsp black onion seeds (optional)
- 2 tbsp chopped coriander (including stalks)
- Vegetable oil for frying

## Directions

1. **Draw out moisture**: Put the carrots in a large bowl, add salt, and mix well.
   1. Leave to stand for 30 minutes to draw the water out of the carrots.
2. **Combine ingredients**: Add all remaining ingredients to the bowl and work them together by hand.
   1. If the mix seems dry, add a splash of cold water to bring it together.
3. **Deep fry**: Heat the oil to 180 degrees.
   1. Deep fry rough tablespoon-sized nuggets in batches until golden brown.
   2. Keep the first batch warm in the oven while the rest cook.
