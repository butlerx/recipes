---
title: Scones
date: 2020-09-16
tags:
  - Dessert
  - Breakfast
cuisine: Irish
yield: 12
prepTime: 5
cookTime: 10
ingredients:
  - 250g self raisingflour
  - 60g unsalted butter
  - 2 TBS of caster sugar
  - 4 TBS of milk
  - 2 small cooking apples, grated
directions:
  - preheat oven to 220 Celcius
  - cut butter in to cubes and keep in freezer before using.
  - Sieve flour in to a bowl
  - rub butter in to flour, until its like breadcrumbs.
  - Add the apple and sugar and mix.
  - Add the milk and mix in to a ball
  - Roll out the mixture on a floured surface.
  - Cut circles out with a cutter and place on greesed baking tray.
  - Brush down with milk.
  - Bake for 10 minutes in the oven, or until golden
  - Allow to cool on a wire rack
---

Family, scones recipes. Best eaten hot
