## Ingredients

- 2 large eggs
- 80 ml milk
- 40 ml cream
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg
- 4 thick slices of brioche
  - stale bread is perfect

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs, milk, cream and vanilla extract together.
1. Place the mixture in a wide shallow dish
1. Dip the bread in the dish and leave for 1-2 minutes
   1. Flip the bread and leave for the same amount of time
1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat
1. Once the butter is foaming place 2 slices of soaked bread in the pan
1. Fry for 3 minutes on each side or until golden and crispy
1. Serve with fresh berries and honey
